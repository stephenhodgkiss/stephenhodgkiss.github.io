About us

Company History

We started as a small team of experienced engineers, developers, and entrepreneurs in 2017. Our goal was to develop a platform that would allow businesses and individuals to securely store and manage data on the blockchain. We believed that this technology would give users more control and privacy when it comes to sharing and exchanging information.

We are a leading provider of innovative blockchain solutions that are designed to revolutionize the way businesses and individuals interact with data. Our mission is to create solutions that are secure, transparent, and reliable for our customers.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

Over the past few years, we have made tremendous progress in our mission. We have expanded our team, created enterprise-level applications, and built out our infrastructure. We are now a global leader in blockchain-based solutions and have customers from all over the world.

At our company, we are committed to creating a better future for everyone. We believe that our technology can help make the world a safer, fairer, and more transparent place. We are proud to be part of the blockchain revolution and look forward to continuing to provide our customers with the best products and services available.

Latest News

Blockchain technology continues to make headlines as more and more companies and governments explore its potential. Recent news includes the launch of Facebook's Libra cryptocurrency, the announcement of Microsoft's new blockchain-based platform, and the introduction of a new UK-based blockchain regulatory body. Additionally, blockchain is being used in the retail industry to increase efficiency and reduce costs, and to enhance security and privacy in the healthcare industry.

Blockchain technology has been making headlines lately as its potential to revolutionize how we store, track, and transact data continues to be explored. One of the latest news stories to emerge is the announcement of a partnership between blockchain technology provider Blockstream and the city of Taipei, Taiwan. The two will be working together to explore and implement blockchain-based solutions in the city's government services. The project will focus on using blockchain technology to create a secure and efficient digital infrastructure for the city and its citizens. In addition, the project aims to create an ecosystem for smart contracts, digital asset management, and personal data management. This partnership is an exciting development for the blockchain industry, and could help usher in a new era of blockchain-based services.

About Stephen Hodgkiss

I'm Stephen Hodgkiss. I'm a web developer living in-between the United Kingdom and S.E. Asia. I am a fan of technology, travel and food. I'm also interested in programming and web development.

Born in the UK, after finishing school I graduated from Technical College with a HND (Higher National Diploma). After working my way up as an Employee of various companies, I went Freelance in 1987. Working both in the UK and locations worldwide, I soon built up my reputation as a very competent developer, being retained by one particular Bank for 15 years.

The last few years I've developed more experience that relates to Blockchain Technology and the way it can empower governments, businesses and customers. This includes the development of blockchain platforms and Cryptocurrency exchanges.
